Hey, you’ve found me. People call me Will o the Wisp. What am I exactly, you may ask.

Some might tell you I am a trickster, here to prank you. Some might say I’m a mischievous, restless young spirit wandering in the realm of humans. Some might even call me treasure light, here to guide you to a wealthy life.

Well I’m just a chemical reaction. When dead bodies trapped in a swamp, they decompose underwater and give birth to me.

I’ll show you the cool stuff I can do.
